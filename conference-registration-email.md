# Finishing Strong in the Age of AI - Registration Website

---

**Subject:** Your Guide to the New Conference Registration Website

**Hello!**

We've created a simple online registration website for **Finishing Strong in the Age of AI** (May 4-5, 2026 in Paradise Island, Bahamas). Here's what you can do with it:

---

## What ATTENDEES Can Do

If you're attending the conference, you can:

1. **Register for FREE** - No registration fee to attend
2. **Enter your basic info** - Name, email, phone, organization
3. **Reserve and pay for meals**:
   - Monday Breakfast - $40
   - Tuesday Breakfast - $40
   - Podium Lunch - $45
4. **Choose which speaker table** you want to sit at during lunch
5. **Sign up for vision screening** (optional eye health check)
6. **Get instant confirmation** via email when you register

---

## What SPEAKERS Can Do

If you're presenting at the conference, you can:

1. **Register as a speaker** - Select "Speaker" when registering
2. **Add your credentials** - List your degrees and titles (Ph.D., M.D., etc.)
3. **Enter your presentation title** - Exactly as it should appear
4. **Pick your preferred time slot** - Choose when you want to present (7 AM - 12 PM)
5. **Request equipment you need**:
   - Your own laptop or use ours
   - Type of microphone (handheld, clip-on, podium)
   - Projector, HDMI cable, internet access
   - Audio for videos or music
6. **List dietary needs** - Food allergies, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.
7. **Request accessibility help** - Wheelchair ramp, cane, walker, service animal, etc.
8. **Add special requests** - Anything else we should know
9. **Get FREE meals** - Speakers don't pay for breakfast or lunch

---

## What CONFERENCE MANAGERS Can Do

If you're managing the conference, you can log into the admin dashboard and:

### **View Everything**
- See how many people registered (total, speakers, attendees)
- See meal counts for ordering food
- See which speaker tables are most popular
- See who signed up for vision screening

### **Manage Registrations**
- Add new registrations manually
- Edit anyone's information
- Delete registrations if needed
- View all speaker details in one place

### **Track Payments**
- See total money owed for meals
- See how much has been paid
- See who still needs to pay
- Mark payments as received with one click
- See payment collection percentage
- **Note:** Speakers are automatically marked as FREE (no payment needed)

### **Export Data**
- Download Excel spreadsheet of all registrations
- Generate PDF report for planning
- Save backup copies of all data

### **Get Planning Help**
- See recommended meal counts (with 10% buffer)
- See seating recommendations for speaker tables
- Get table planning suggestions
